# SmartOps Product Documentation Prompt

You are a Senior Product Manager, Solution Architect, Mobile App Architect, UX Designer, and Startup CTO.

I am building a mobile-first business management platform called **SmartOps**.

## Product Vision

SmartOps is an all-in-one business operating system designed primarily for small businesses, with the ability to scale to medium and large businesses over time.

The goal is to help business owners manage daily operations from a single application instead of using multiple tools.

The platform should start as a mobile app (Android and iOS) and later expand into a web application and full SaaS platform.

---

## Target Users

### Phase 1

Small businesses including:

* Retail shops
* Grocery stores
* Manufacturing units
* Service businesses
* Agencies
* Workshops
* Distributors
* Restaurants and cafes
* Construction contractors
* Local businesses with 1–100 employees

### Future Phases

* Medium-sized businesses
* Multi-branch businesses
* Enterprises
* Franchises
